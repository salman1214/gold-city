Islamabad Gold City

Islamabad Gold City is a project of IGC PVT LIMITED, launching an international standard project near New Islamabad International Airport located at beautiful and easily accessible location of Islamabad with just 5 mins drive from Airport and 10mins drive from Motorway and CPEC. It would hardly take 30 mins to reach Islamabad downtown or main areas of Rawalpindi and Taxila. Islamabad Gold City is located near beautiful mountains which will help you to make your dreams come true. At IGC, we are planning to provide world class living facilities with international standards for schools, colleges, universities, hospitals, and commercial markets. The tentative project launching date is January 2024 and we are planning to proceed with development by End of year 2024. Islamabad Gold City is a steadily growing real estate and land developer company from the experienced and diversified members. We are a major real estate developer company in Pakistan, committed to going above and beyond to provide the highest level of services to local and international clients. We have diversified experience of land development, property consultancy and land acquisition with experienced team members where you can expect trusted and expert advice from our consultants.

WHY ISLAMABAD GOLD CITY

Islamabad Gold City is a tandem of luxurious architecture and comfort at one place and it will be one of the finest places to live within Islamabad and Rawalpindi premises with having direct entrance from the New Islamabad International Airport and located near Motorway, CPEC and new Ring Road of Islamabad and Rawalpindi. We are trying to bring bigger international standard universities, schools, colleges, hospitals and 5-star hotels to make it a dream place to live.

AMENITIES & FACILITIES

EASY ACCESS

Islamabad Gold City is an unmatchable society in terms of easy access from the Rawalpindi, Islamabad, Taxila, Wah Cantt, Fateh Jhang areas having an advantage of being situated right next to the motorway, CPEC and right behind the new Islamabad International Airport. It is also accessible through the new Ring Road of twin cities.

MODERN DEVELOPMENT & LATEST INFRASTRUCTURE

Islamabad Gold City has planned to develop the society with international standards by offering all underground piping and wiring of electricity and sewerage with the utmost luxury experience for our residents. We are planning to deploy sector-wise sewerage treatment plants to ensure the green environment of the society. Underground water supply network with tubewells and overhead water storage facilities.

ROAD NETWORK

Islamabad Gold City has planned to develop a wide road network infrastructure in the society with 400ft main Boulevard which will cover the complete society. The secondary road network which will cover the side skirts of the society comprises 200ft and 150ft wide double roads for the resident’s accessibility. The minimum street size is 40ft for small houses and up to 80ft wider streets in residential areas for the convenience and easy commute of the residents and business community.

SECURITY

Islamabad Gold City will provide dedicated security model for its residents by ensuring to monitor each corner of the society and manage 24/7 security for its residents. Our highly trained security professionals will be well equipped and stationed all over the society and perform regular patrols throughout the society.

GREEN AREAS & PARKS

Islamabad Gold City is more focused towards the Green Environment to support the overall culture of Pakistan and by considering the beautiful atmosphere of Twin Cities we have spared ample amount of open and green spaces for children play areas, walking and jogging tracks, open air gym, amusement parks, water parks. relaxing parks with beautiful and amazing lighting experience which meant for residents’ social and recreational activities as well as keeping the environment green, healthy, and pollution-free. Islamabad Gold City makes sure that our residents live as a thriving community.

HOSPITALS & NEIGHBORHOOD CLINICS

Islamabad Gold City has planned to develop all types of medical and health care facility units for its residents which includes but not limited to specialized and primary healthcare facilities.
