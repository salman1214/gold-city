Product Description

Mahaana Munaafa term deposit provides you the comfort of liquidity along with a security of your money being in safe hands.

Minimum investment

Rs. 5000/-.

Instruments Issuance

Rs. 1000/-.

Term Deposit

Fixed for 6 Months, 12 Months, 2 Years, 3 Years, 4 Years & 5 Years.

  • Minimum investment of Rs. 5000/-.
  • Instruments will be issued in multiples of Rs. 1000/-.
  • Profit will be paid on monthly basis in customer's account maintained with the bank.
  • Term Deposit may be fixed for 6 Months, 12 Months, 2 Years, 3 Years, 4 Years & 5 Years.
  • *No penalty for premature payment will be levied in case of premature payment of deposits
  • Profit payment is subject to tax deduction.

Note: * In case of pre-mature encashement of instrument, rate of previous term completed will be applicable.
