INTRODUCTION

Gold Farms is a project of Gold Hillview Farm Houses Private Limited, which is under the umbrella of Islamabad Gold City. We are launching an international standard Agro Farm Houses on scenic hilly landscape located near New Islamabad International Airport. The location is easily accessible from Islamabad and Rawalpindi. It is on 5 mins drive from Airport, while Motorway and CPEC are at 8-10 mins drive. Islamabad downtown and central Rawalpindi, Wah Cantt, Taxila and Fatehjhang are at 25 mins distance. The executive living of GOLD FARMS will help you to make your dreams come true. It contains world class living facilities including international standards Golf Course, Community Clubs, easily accessible schools, colleges, universities, hospitals, and commercial markets. The tentative launching date of project is January 2024. Gold Farms is a rapidly growing real estate arena under supervision of highly experienced members which aims at providing quality services to local and international clients.
